Our Vision
A Generation Transformed by Christ
Christ Impact Outreach exists to see billions of young people encounter the gospel of Jesus Christ and become Spirit-empowered problem solvers in their nations.
  • We reach young people both online and offline — through crusades, digital gospel campaigns, and direct outreach wherever they are.
  • Every young person we reach receives a Bible and a book that teaches them to partner with God and the Holy Spirit as their helper.
  • We believe saved young people become the greatest solution to every problem facing the world today.
  • Our vision is to reach billions — one child, one Bible, one book, one transformed life at a time.
